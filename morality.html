"If Kṛṣṇa is satisfied, then it is morality"

Posted on : 18th March, 2025

Our standard of morality and immorality is to see whether Kṛṣṇa is satisfied. If Kṛṣṇa is satisfied, then it is morality. If Kṛṣṇa is dissatisfied, then it is immoral.

~ Lecture on Bhāgavat-Gītā 2.26-27 -- London, August 29, 1973.
