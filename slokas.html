Slokas Applicable to Srila Prabhupad#1

tvayopabhukta-srag-gandha- vāso-’laṅkāra-carcitāḥ

ucchiṣṭa-bhojino dāsās tava māyāṁ jayema hi

Simply by decorating ourselves with the garlands, fragrant oils, clothes and ornaments that You have already enjoyed, and by eating the remnants of Your meals, we, Your servants, will indeed conquer Your illusory energy. (SB 11.6.46)

As Uddhava says that by getting krsna's remnants and other enjoyed transendental items, we his servants will indeed conquer his material illusory energy which is very strong. Same way Srila prabhupad's remnants, instructions, glance, even wind coming from the direction where he and his devotees are standing, purifies his devoted servants.

tāṅra upadeśa-mantre piśācī palāya

(Cc. m. 22.15)

By Srila Prabhupad's instructions the witch of maya flies away. One can conquer Maya by constantly remembering Krsna's and Srila Prabhupad's lotus feets, Prabhupad writes in purport; "Even if Your illusory energy tries to attack us, my Lord, we will very easily conquer her by our powerful weapons, which are the remnants of Your foodstuffs, clothes, ornaments, and so on. In other words, we will easily conquer over māyā by kṛṣṇa-prasādam, and not by useless speculation and mental concoction.” Yes among all the weapons this is most powerful _caranopasnastra_ the weapon of loving service to Lotus feets of sri guru and gauranga!. One should not waste time in concoctions and mental speculations but keep on rendering service.

guror hareś caraṇopāsanāstro jahi vyalīkaṁ svayam ātma-moṣam

(SB 5.11.17)

Please try to conquer this mind by the weapon of service to the lotus feet of the spiritual master and of the Supreme Personality of Godhead. So that by this service we will get kripa as Krsna Prasad and Krsna kripa murti prasad means Prabhupad's Prasad, by the strength of these great weapons we the eternal servants of Srila Prabhupad will Conquer Maya.
