A rough hit but a soft landing

Posted on : 4th September, 2024

Srila Prabhupada once recalled with great clarity, an accident that occurred when he was very young, and his family was travelling in a bullock cart. After all those decades, he still distinctly remembered flying through the air when the wheels hit a stone and collapsed. Baby Abhay was the only one unhurt, landing on soft grass.
