"Śrīla Prabhupāda, you have given us everything"

Śrīla Prabhupāda, you have given us everything, and you have given us the perfect example of how to follow the ācārya. The specific program and methods for preaching Krsna consciousness and achieving the goal of human life have been given by you. There is no necessity of additions or deletions. If we follow you, we will have no problems, and we can also become fearless at the lotus feet of Śrī Chaitanya Mahāprabhu by your mercy.

~ Rūpa-vilāsa Dāsa, Bhaktivedanta Manor Vyās Pujā offering, 2009.
