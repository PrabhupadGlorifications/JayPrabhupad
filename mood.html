The Mood and Mission of Śrīla Prabhupāda

Śrīla Prabhupāda is the very life of ISKCON, and service to him is the sure means of success, both for the members of ISKCON and the Institution as a whole. But what exactly did Śrīla Prabhupāda want and expect from his followers? What were his plans and his hopes? How relevant are his instructions today? Can we change Or neglect them?

Investigation of these vital questions makes The Mood and Mission of Śrīla Prabhupāda essential reading for all devotees who wish to serve Śrīla Prabhupāda in the best possible way.
