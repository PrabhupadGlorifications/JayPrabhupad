Cooking Oil

Revatinandana :One day I was cooking in the temple kitchen with Aniruddha. Aniruddha came back in from seeing Prabhupada and said 'Prabhupada just told me that we are not supposed to cook with mustard seeds or mustard or fenugreek any more.' Prabhupada wanted us to use simple spices, cumin seeds, chilies, asafoetida, as well as simple techniques. He specifically mentioned not using mustard seed and mustard oil, as well as fenugreek. In India later on, he once remarked, "My God-brothers generally prefer mustard oil, but I like ghee."

Ref- Memories Anecdotes of Modern day saint - Volume 1 by Siddhanta Dasa
