"He Opened the Door"

Srila Prabhupād organised so many festivals, printed so many books and magazines, constructed many beautiful temples, arranged Dhāma-parikramā. He sent preachers to all corners of the world, to every town and village, making arrangements for preaching the science of Krsna Consciousness. He opened the door for one and all, regardless of whether one is pāpi or tāpi, the most degraded, sinful person or the most elevated person. That is his causeless mercy, supreme mercy, incomparable and uncommon mercy.

~ Ref~ HH Gour Govind Swami, pg. 16, Krsna Kripa Sri murti. Tattva-vicāra Publications.
