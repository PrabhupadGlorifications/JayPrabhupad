'Lip service – "I love… I love…"

“I love Prabhupada! I love Prabhupada!” Do you? Prabhupada did not just want lip service – “I love… I love…” He wanted that love to be translated into action. Srila Prabhupada wanted us to become the executors of that will. I would say that this is one of the principle wills – protection of his institution. That has to be done by cooperating with each other and all the generations to come in the future.

~ HH Loknāth Swāmi Mahārāja, on HDG AC Bhaktivedanta swami Srila Prabhupād's Disappearance day 2018, Vrindāvan.
