Quoting Prabhupād = Quoting Śāstrās

Posted on : 12th June, 2025

Quoting Prabhupād's Purports is as authorized as quoting from the original Sanskrit śāstrās. The words of the ācāryās are in one sense even more valuable than the original śāstrās, because without the ācāryā's explanations, ordinary people would not be able to understand the meaning of śāstrā.

~ Jaya Srila Prabhupād, book by HH Bhaktivikāsa Swāmi Mahārāja

Jay Śrīla Prabhupāda!

Note : Saccidānand Thākur Bhaktivonde also speaks same regarding Ācāryā Vāni and revealed scriptures in his Book “Tattva Viveka”.
