How should we prepare ourselves for war?

Devotee : So Prabhupada, is there something we should do to prepare ourselves for this disaster?

Prabhupada : What?

Devotee : This coming war.

Prabhupada : You should simply prepare for chanting Hare Kṛṣṇa.

Devotee : That's all?

Prabhupada : That's all.

~ Srila Prabhupada Conversation, Mayapur, April 4, 1975
