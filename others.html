All others will die

Posted on : 8th November, 2024

"Sambandha” jāniyā yebā jivana-yuddhakare Seita’ vānciyā thāke āra saba mare"

"Whoever knows the spirit soul's eternal relationship with Lord Krsna and fights the battle of life for his sake will survive, while all others will die."

~ HDG AC Bhaktivedanta swami Prabhupad, section 10 under title Vrindāvane Bhajana, Book - A Shower of Divine Compassion.
