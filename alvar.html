Tirumangai Ālvar and Shrila Prabhupād

Tirumangai Ālvar of the Sri Sampradāya engaged thieves, liars, cheaters and rogues in the service of the Lord. He purified their sinful propensities by engaging them in devotional service, and with their help he did tremendous work for establishing Krsna consciousness. Similarly, Srīla Prabhupāda accepted all kinds of questionable, rejected characters, made them saintly persons, and spread the message of Lord Caitanya through them. Jaya Srīla Prabhupāda
