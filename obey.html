Obeying Srīla Prabhupāda

Posted on : 4th October, 2024

Remembrance of Srīla Prabhupāda, love for Srīla Prabhupāda, praise of Srīla Prabhupāda, and service to Srīla Prabhupāda must all be based on Obeying Srīla Prabhupāda. Otherwise, such sentiments are superficial, hypocritical, and devoid of the potency to fulfil Srīla Prabhupāda's mission of spreading Krsna consciousness all over the world.

~ HH Srīpād Bhaktīvikāsa Swāmi Mahārāja.
