In the material world there is no love

Posted on : 11th April, 2024

In the material world there is no love. Because everyone is directed by lust. The so-called love... A young boy is trying to love a young girl, or young girl is trying to love a young..., but the background is lust. There is no love. It is simply a show of love.

~ Lecture on Bhāgavat-Gītā 3.31-43 -- Los Angeles, January 1, 1969.
