The Real Jagat-Guru

Posted on : 10th November, 2024

In Glorification of Sriman Mahaprabhu Sri Prabhodananda saraswati thakur said;

prema namadbhutarthah sravana-patha-gatah kasya namnam mahimnah

ko vetta kasya vrndavana-vipina-maha madhurisu pravesah

ko va janati radham parama-rasa-chamatkara-madhurya-simlam

ekas chaitanya-chandrah parama-karunaya sarvam avischakara

"Would anyone have heard that 'prema' divine love of God is the highest destination? Would anyone have known the glory of the Holy Name? Would anyone have had access to the kadamba forests of Vrndavan, filled with unfathomable sweetness? Would anyone have known Sri Varsabhanavi devi Sri Radha, the highest worshippable Deity, who is the resplendent embodiment of superb excellence of sweet ecstatic love? (Ekas chaitanya chandra parama karunaya sarvam avischakara) Only, Sri Chaitanya-chandra has revealed all this, by manifesting His supreme magnanimous pastimes." {Sri chaitanya chandramrita, 10.130}
