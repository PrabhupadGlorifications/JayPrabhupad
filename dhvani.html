Jaya Prabhupāda!

Posted on : 5th April, 2025

Jaya Prabhupāda! this is not just a slogan or a part of Jaya dhvani. This is a roar of lions, who intent to drive away all ignorance and forgetfulness about the supreme and establish the original truth that Krsna is Supreme Personality of Godhead and his loving service is the only worth doing action, everything else is waste, sinful. For the serious and real devotees who are really into this extraordinary dhara of lord Chaitanya, this Jaya Prabhupāda! is their whole existence, life and soul. We Live for Prabhupāda and we are alive because of Prabhupāda otherwise we all would have spiritually dead in this horrible age of Kali.

Jay Om Visnupād Paramhamsa Parivrajakācarya Yugācārya Jagatguru Vishwaguru Astottara sata Srī Srīmad HDG A.C Bhaktivedānta Swāmi Srīla Prabhupād ki Jaya!!
