Sajjana सज्जन

The word 'Sajjana' is made up of two parts viz, 'Sat' and Jana, 'Sat' is 'Godhead' or the 'Absolute Truth. This is in accordance with the śruti 'om tatsat' Sat' means the Ever-Existent, the Unchangeable lord, the One Spirit and Harmony Himself. The 'Sajjana' is one who belongs to and serves the Sat(Krsna),.......... The word Sajjana' is not really narrow in its denotation. To him who possesses the clearest spiritual vision all living beings without exception are Sajjanas'.

- From the Original Foreword By His Divine Grace Srīla Bhaktisiddhānta Sarasvatī Thākura Prabhupād, june 1927 Harmonist Ed. 25.1

The one who is having knowledge about Lord and is eager to serve the supreme is the real sajjana all others are durjanas. One is not a devotee of lord because he is a good ethical person, but he is a good person because he is a devotee.
