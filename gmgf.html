"He can go on glorifying śrīla Prabhupāda forever"

Posted on : 2nd June, 2025

"As he (Bhaktivikāsa swāmi) can go on glorifying śrīla bhaktisiddhanta saraswati thakur forever, he can go on glorifying śrīla Prabhupāda forever"

~ HH DANVIR SWAMI (on the book Mood and Mission of srila Prabhupada)

Jay Śrīla Prabhupāda!
