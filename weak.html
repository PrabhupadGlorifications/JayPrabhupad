O Lord and Master! When will such mercy fall to this one who is weak and devoid of intelligence?


kabe heno kripa, labhiya e jana,

kritartha hoibe, natha!

sakti-buddhi-hina, ami ati dina,

koro’ more atma-satha


jogyata-vicare, kichu nahi pai,

tomara karuna—sara

karuna na hoile, kandiya kandiya,

prana na rakhibo ara


O Lord and Master! When will such mercy fall to this one who is weak and devoid of intelligence? Allow me to be with you. If you examine me, you will find no qualities. Your mercy is all that I am made of. If you are not merciful unto me, I can only weep, and I will not be able to maintain my life.

~ Thakur Bhaktivinode
