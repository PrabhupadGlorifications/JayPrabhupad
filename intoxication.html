Approved intoxication for devotees

The book distribution in Los Angeles during the six day period is transcendental samadhi. They are working in trance, not on the material platform. No common man can work so hard, it is not possible. Working without sleep means no death. Sleeping is dead condition. "Jiv jago, jiva jago, gauracanda bole, kota nidra yao maya-pisacira kole." Your book distribution is really intoxication.

(Srila Prabhupada; letter to Ramesvara, 12 January, 1976)

Jay Śrīla Prabhupāda!
