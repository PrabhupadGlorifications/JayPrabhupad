"From time immemorial they are against us, we are part of Revolution."

Srila Bhaktisiddhānta Sarasvati Thakura once offered a telling reply to a provocative question: Do all panditas in India accept Srimad Bhāgavatam, or do scholars of other schools stand opposed to its authority? His characteristically straightforward response nonetheless surprises : "There is no deliberation in the world which is not against Srimad Bhagavatam. All the different currents of thoughts of jvas averse to God from time immemorial are against it." In that same mood, Srila Prabhupāda branded his books revolutionary, for they are "an attempt to glorify Krsna, the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Nothing more. There is no literary career." (Srila Prabhupād Lecture on Srimad- Bhāgavatam 1.8.20, Mayāpur, September 30, 1974)

~ Bhurijana Dāsa in Book "The Backward Glance"
