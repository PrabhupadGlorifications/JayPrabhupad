SRILA PRABHUPĀD in ISKCON Thane

Posted on : 16th October, 2024

ॐ Varnāsrama Dharma Punar pratishthācāryam Saranam aham prapadye


I shall Surrender unto the one who has reestablished the varnāsarama dharma for the benefit of all the classes and orders.


Mahāparākramam prabhum kali kalmasha nāshanam

Ati visesa guna sāgaram vande ham shāstra panditam.


Shāntam shāshvatam prabhum sri Rādhikā-Nāyaka priyam

Rajani garbha sambhutam Vande ham dina pāvanam


Bahu yojana vitaram mahāpāpaga nāshanam

suddha sattvam sarva shreshtham vande ham Bhakta Raksakam


Gaurapriyam gaura bhaktam gaura tattva

Pracārakam gaurāshritam gaura vatsalam vande ham gaura nandanam


Sarva desa digvijayam Rādhā-Dāmodara priyam

vajra pushpa hrdaya dharam vande ham sisya sevitam


Translation :

I offer my obeisances unto him, who is master, who is bold and courageous, who vanquishes the impurities of the age of kali, who is an ocean of excellent qualities, and who is an expert in the scriptures. I offer my obeisances, who is peaceful, who is eternal, who is master, who is dear to the Lord of Srimati Rādhārāni, who has appeared as the son of mother Rajani, and who is a saviour of the fallen. Who has crossed many miles, who destroys great amount of sinfulness, who is in the mode of pure goodness, who is best among all, and who is the protector of devotees. Who is dear to Lord chaitanya, who is a devotee of Lord chaitanya, who has taken shelter of Lord chaitanya, who is devoted to Lord chaitanya, and one who is son of Sri Gaura mohan de. Who is victorious in all Directions in preaching the message of Lord Chaitanya, who is very dear to Sri Rādhā-Dāmodara, whose heart is as strong as a thunderbolt and as tender as a flower, and who is served by his disciples.
